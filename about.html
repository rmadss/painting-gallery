About

Painting since 2017.

A little about me

I've been painting since around 2017, mostly just as something I love doing. Started with portraits of musicians — Beatles, Jimi Hendrix, that kind of thing — and it kind of snowballed from there.

Lately I've been branching out into places and scenes, which has been fun. No formal training, just a lot of time, a lot of YouTube, and a lot of paint. Thanks for taking the time to look through the work — it genuinely means a lot.

How I work

Most paintings start with a photo reference — usually something I shot myself or a photo I really love. I'll sketch it out loosely first, then block in the big shapes, then just keep refining until it feels done (or until I stop messing with it, which is sometimes the same thing).

I work in oil mostly. There's something about the slowness of it that I like — it forces you to commit to decisions instead of just hitting undo.
